Rules

To submit a record on this list, you must have:

  • Achieved the record without the use of hacks.

    Unlimited FPS is allowed, Physics Bypass is prohibited. Usage of Noclip Accuracy may lead to an invalid completion if the endscreen shows more than 0 deaths even though Noclip was not used.

  • Achieved the record on the level that is listed on the site.

    Please check the level ID before you submit a record. You are allowed to beat the Low Detail Mode Versions listed in the "Pemonlist LDM" List ingame. It is prohibited to change the levels in any way.

When recording a completion:

  • The recording must also show the player hit the end trigger AND the endscreen with the time shown on said endscreen, or the completion will be invalidated.

  • The recording must have clicks for extreme demon completions.

  • The completion may not include any exploits (Secret Ways, Bugs, Pause Buffering) - Small skips are fine.

  • The record may be achieved with the Globed mod active. (Subject to change depending on what gets added in the future of the mod)

Keep in mind:

  • The Usage of the "Hitbox on Death" Hack is prohibited for memory levels or levels where it serves a clear advantage over not using it.

  • The Usage of the "Click Between Frames" Mod is prohibited due to its buggy nature with platformers and significantly altering the game's physics.
